Suggestions for your next visit

Some of the most popular destination highlights for when you visit Eureka Springs include the following places around:

  • The historic 1886 Crescent Hotel & Spa
  • Onyx Cave Park
  • The St. Elizabeth Catholic Church
  • Quigley's Castle

Eureka Springs is a lovely community within the state of Arkansas set within the Ozark Mountains, home to a wide array of beautiful locales as described above,
a rich history and plenty of opportunities for anyone for tourism or that perfect vacation destination.

Did you know?

Eureka Springs is home to 60 naturally occuring hot springs within the place, the main reason for the settlement of the city.
